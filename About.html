Socially Aware Youth

ABOUT US

Socially Aware Youth (SAY) is a special interest club at Alhambra High School made up of students who share a common passion for learning and engaging in current affairs around the globe. Our goal is to not only shed light onto these social, economic, and political issues, but to act upon our visions through perseverance and teamwork. Join us in making a difference within our school, community, and ultimately, the world.
